Harshvardhan Uppaluru

I am a third year Ph.D. student at the Aerospace & Mechanical Engineering Department, part of the College of Engineering at University of Arizona advised by Prof. Hossein Rastgoftar. I am interested in working at the intersection of machine learning, control theory and continuum mechanics to develop real-time and safe algorithms for multi-robot systems especially quadrotors.

Prior to pursuing Ph.D., I completed my Masters in Robotics Engineering from University of Maryland - College Park. I have a Bachelors degree in Mechatronics Engineering from SRM University (Currently SRM Institute of Science & Technology).

In my spare time, I play cricket and watch soccer. My favorite soccer team is Arsenal.

I am currently looking for internships in SUMMER 2022.
