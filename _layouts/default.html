Harshvardhan Uppaluru

I am a fourth year Ph.D. student in the Scalable Move And Resilient Transversability (SMART) Lab at University of Arizona, advised by Prof. Hossein Rastgoftar. SMART Lab is associated with Aerospace & Mechanical Engineering Department and College of Engineering. I am minoring in Systems and Industrial Engineering advised by Prof. Erfan Yazdandoost Hamedani.

Prior to pursuing Ph.D., I completed my Masters in Robotics Engineering from University of Maryland - College Park. I have a Bachelors degree in Mechatronics Engineering from SRM University (Currently SRM Institute of Science & Technology).

Apart from research, I love to teach! I play cricket and watch soccer on weekends. Arsenal is my favorite soccer team. In addition, I completed four levels of cricket umpiring: USACUA Preliminary, USACUA Final, WICUA Final, and WICUA Oral & Practical. I have umpired 85 games in total for Minor League Cricket (MiLC), Washington Cricket League (WCL), Arizona Cricket Association (ACA) and Washington Youth Cricket League (WYCL).
